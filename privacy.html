Privacy Policy

PinForge Studio

Last updated: February 6, 2025

1. Introduction

PinForge Studio ("we", "our", "the application") is a content management tool designed to help creators schedule and publish visual content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Our application may collect the following types of information:

  • Account credentials: OAuth tokens required to connect to Pinterest on your behalf. These are stored locally and securely.
  • Content data: Pin titles, descriptions, images, and scheduling preferences that you create within the application.
  • Analytics data: Performance metrics (impressions, saves, clicks) retrieved from Pinterest's API for pins you have published.

3. How We Use Information

All information is used solely for the purpose of providing the application's core functionality:

  • Publishing and scheduling pins to your Pinterest account
  • Displaying performance analytics for your content
  • Managing your content calendar and publishing queue

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your own device or server. We do not transmit your data to any third-party servers beyond the APIs required for the application to function (Pinterest API).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The application communicates only with Pinterest's official API to perform actions you have authorized.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. API tokens and credentials are stored securely and are never exposed in logs or user interfaces.

7. Your Rights

You may disconnect your Pinterest account at any time through the application settings. Upon disconnection, all stored OAuth tokens are deleted. You may also delete all local data by removing the application's database.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub profile.
